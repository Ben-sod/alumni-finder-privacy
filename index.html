Privacy Policy for Alumni Finder

Last updated: [Current Date]

Data Collection

Alumni Finder stores all data locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. No data is sent to external servers.

What Data We Collect

When you use the "Record Alumni" feature, the extension stores:

  • Prospect names
  • Current and previous companies
  • Job titles
  • LinkedIn profile URLs

How Data Is Used

The extension only accesses data from LinkedIn and Salesforce pages you're actively viewing. All data is stored locally and can be exported as CSV files.

Data Security

All data is stored locally on your device. The extension does not send any data to external servers or share information with third parties.
